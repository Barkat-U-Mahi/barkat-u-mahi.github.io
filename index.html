About me

Hello, I'm Barkat Ullah Mahi, a student, gamer, photographer, and programmer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I completed my SSC examination at Motijheel Model High School & College and my HSC examination at Khilgaon Govt High School. Currently, I am pursuing a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Daffodil Institute of IT (DIIT).
